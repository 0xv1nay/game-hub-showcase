ABOUT ARCADIA

Preserving the golden age of arcade gaming, one pixel at a time.

OUR STORY

Arcadia was born from a simple passion: the love of classic arcade games. In an era of hyper-realistic graphics and complex gameplay mechanics, we believe there's still magic in the simplicity of retro gaming.

Our journey began when a group of gaming enthusiasts decided to create a platform where anyone could experience the joy of classic arcade games without the hassle of downloads, installations, or subscriptions.

Today, Arcadia hosts a carefully curated collection of timeless classics – from the block-stacking perfection of Tetris to the maze-running excitement of Pac-Man. Each game has been lovingly adapted to work seamlessly in modern browsers while preserving the authentic feel of the originals.

OUR MISSION

We're on a mission to preserve gaming history and make it accessible to everyone. Our goals are simple:

  • Keep classic games alive and accessible for future generations
  • Provide a free, ad-free gaming experience for all
  • Celebrate the artistry and innovation of early game developers
  • Build a community of retro gaming enthusiasts
